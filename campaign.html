🏙️ IRL Tactics take it off the internet
📋
Craigslist Gigs
Post in the city's Gigs section: "Need 5 people to hold signs and hand out flyers near [office/event location] for 2 hours — $40 cash each." IRL presence for under $250.
📰
Flyers & Posters
Print 500 flyers at FedEx for ~$30. Post around their office building, at relevant events, and on public community boards. QR code links to this campaign page.
🎨
Sticker Campaign
Order 100 custom stickers from Sticker Mule (~$40). Deploy around relevant venues, bulletin boards, and public spaces. The more places, the more impressions.
🚌
Outdoor Ads
Place ads on bus shelters, benches, or billboards near their office or regular event venues. Local OOH companies sell short runs at reasonable rates.
📺
Media Tips
Submit a tip to investigative TV reporters, podcasters, and bloggers. Journalists and content creators love a good scandal — give them the story pre-packaged with screenshots and receipts.
🎤
Public Appearances
If the target speaks at conferences, investor meetups, or public events — show up. Ask pointed questions during Q&A. Have someone recording. Post immediately.
